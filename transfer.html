How to make a Transfer?

Learn more about our application process and requirements

Transfer Admission

Students transferring from another school may apply for admission depending on space availability. The school administration carefully reviews each transfer request to ensure the student is placed in an appropriate learning environment.

Transfer Process

Step 1

Submit a Transfer Application Form. (Collect at the school office.)

Step 2

Provide academic records from the previous school.

Step 3

The student must complete a placement interview.

Step 4

The school will notify parents of the admission decision.

Transfer Requirements

  • Completed Transfer Form
  • Application Form
  • Previous School Most Recent Report Card
  • Release Letter from Previous School
  • Copy of Birth Certificate Copy
  • Copy of Immunization Record (Cover of booklet and pages 14 & 15)
  • One Passport Photograph
  • Proof of Address
  • Copy of parent/guardian ID and TRN
