Enrollment Overview

Mona Heights Primary School provides a nurturing learning environment where students are encouraged to grow academically, socially, and personally. Our admissions process ensures that each student is placed in an environment that supports their development and success.

Admission Process

Step 1: Submit Application

Parents/guardians must complete the official school application form. (DO NOT CARRY ANY DOCUMENT UNLESS INSTRUCTED TO DO SO).

Step 2: Document Review

The school administration reviews all submitted application forms to verify eligibility and ensure the application is complete.

Step 3: Student Assessment

Students are required to participate in a brief interview to determine their academic level.

Step 4: Admission Decision

After a breif period parents will be notified of the admission decision. Accepted students will receive enrollment instructions.

Admission Requirements

    Please note that there should be two (2) copies of each

  • Completed Application Form
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Copy of Immunization Record (Cover of booklet and pages 14 & 15)
  • Medical Form
  • Previous School Report (if applicable)
  • Two Passport-Sized Photographs
  • Proof of Address
  • Copy of parent/guardian ID and TRN
