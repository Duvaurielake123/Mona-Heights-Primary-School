Our Academics

Building strong foundations for lifelong learning

Curriculum Overview

Mona Heights Primary School follows the national curriculum of Jamaica, providing a balanced and comprehensive education designed to develop students academically, socially, and emotionally.

Core Subjects

Mathematics

Developing problem-solving skills and logical thinking through engaging and interactive lessons.

Language Arts

Strengthening reading, writing, grammar, and communication skills to build confident learners.

Science

Encouraging curiosity and critical thinking through hands-on experiments and discovery.

Social Studies

Exploring history, geography, and civic responsibility to develop informed citizens.

Ability

Measures students'cognitive reasning, critical thinking, and information processing skills rather than their rote curriculum knowledge.

Spanish

Equipping students with language skills and cultural awareness to enhance communication and global understanding.

Assessment & Evaluation

Students are assessed through continuous evaluation including class participation, assignments, quizzes, projects, and examinations. Our goal is to ensure steady progress and identify areas for improvement.
