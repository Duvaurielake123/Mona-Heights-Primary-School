About Our School

Virtute et Labore - With Courage and Effort

Our History

Mona Heights Primary was established in 1967 to meet the needs of providing Primary Education to the community of Mona Heights and its environs. Since its inception, Mona Heights Primary School has been a standard- bearer in the delivery of Primary education, not only for its immediate environment, but also for the entire island of Jamaica. Our students have been trail-blazers in academics, culture, sports, arts and numerous other areas of endeavors.


Mission Statement

The mission of Mona Heights Primary School is to develop a learning center that is digitally enhanced - designed to facilitate wholistic learning experiences and harmonious social interaction in which each child has the opportunity to realize his/her true natural potential.

"At Mona Heights Primary School, we believe that every child has the potential to succeed when given the right guidance and support."

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the school of choice, placing emphasis on the holistic development of each pupil through academic excellence, vocational training and co-curricular activities in an enriched ICT- driven environment.


School Motto

The official motto of the Mona Heights Primary School is 'Virtute et Labore', 'With Courage and Effort'. Which depicts the values and virtues we instill in our students in achieving a meaningful life and purpose.


Our Creed

We believe we can do all things and become anything we set our minds to. We are responsible for ourselves and our actions. We have compassion for others and we will create a better world. If we believe and work hard, we will achieve.


Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Excellence
  • Teamwork
  • Responsibility
